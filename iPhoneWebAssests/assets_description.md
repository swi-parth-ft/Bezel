# Bezel Studio: iPhone Web Assets Description

This document outlines the purpose and meaning behind each high-fidelity mockup in the `iPhoneWebAssests` directory.

## 1. iCloud Syncing (`icloud.png`, `icloud-bg.png`, `icloud-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Showcases an iPhone and iPad side-by-side, both displaying the same active project.
**Key Message:** Highlights seamless continuity; users can initiate their design work on an iPhone and transition smoothly to an iPad to refine or finalize it.

## 2. Dynamic Backgrounds (`backgrounds.png`, `backgrounds-bg.png`, `backgrounds-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Features four iPhones demonstrating the versatility of canvas backdrops.
**Key Message:** Displays a variety of options including procedural patterns, smooth gradients, playful emoji patterns, and custom image uploads.

## 3. Bezel AI (`bezelAI.png`, `bezelAI-bg.png`, `bezelAI-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Features a screenshot of the conversational AI interface in action.
**Key Message:** Demonstrates the power of Apple Intelligence; users can simply describe their vision (e.g., "Redesign the canvas," "Add bounce animations") and the AI executes the commands instantly.

## 4. Canvas Management (`canvasMangement.png`, `canvasMangement-bg.png`, `canvasMangement-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Shows a grid view of multiple active canvases within a single project.
**Key Message:** Highlights the organizational power of Bezel Studio, allowing users to manage complex multi-screen app marketing campaigns in one unified workspace.

## 5. Image Playground & Stickers (`imagePlayground.png`, `imagePlayground-bg.png`, `imagePlayground-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Showcases on-device sticker generation and background removal.
**Key Message:** Leverages Apple Intelligence's Image Playground to create custom, AI-generated stickers and assets directly within the app.

## 6. Layer Hierarchy (`layers.png`, `layers-bg.png`, `layers-ipad.png`)
**Description:** A detailed view of the canvas layer stack.
**Key Message:** Demonstrates precision control over object depth, allowing users to intuitively drag, drop, and rearrange layers for perfect compositions.

## 7. Pro Layouts & Multi-Device Frames (`prolayput.png`, `prolayput-bg.png`, `prolayput-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Displays a diverse mix of iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch frames within a single canvas.
**Key Message:** Showcases the ability to create complex marketing assets that represent the entire Apple hardware ecosystem simultaneously.

## 8. Canvas Motion & Keyframes (`CanvasMotion.png`, `CanvasMotion-bg.png`, `CanvasMotion-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Showcases the intuitive motion editor and animation timeline.
**Key Message:** Highlights the advanced animation engine, allowing users to add keyframes with sophisticated easing (Ease In/Out, Bounce, Smooth) for professional App Store Previews.

## 9. Typography (`typography.png`, `typography-bg.png`, `typography-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Showcases a variety of premium font styles and typography options.
**Key Message:** Highlights the extensive font library and customization options for text elements.

## 10. Translate Assets (`translate.png`, `translate-bg.png`, `translate-ipad.png`)
**Description:** Demonstrates the on-device translation capabilities for screenshots.
**Key Message:** Emphasizes the ease of localizing app previews and marketing materials for global audiences.
