Device Frame Feature

Clean Status Bar for framed screenshots and recordings.

Clean Status Bar replaces messy captured status bar content in supported iPhone frame media with a clean Apple-style 9:41 treatment, so screenshots and app preview clips feel presentation-ready before export.

9:41 polish Screenshots Screen recordings

Why It Matters

Raw captures often carry messy, inconsistent status bars.

App Store screenshots need to look intentional. When the frame media is supported, Bezel Studio can clean the status area inside the mockup instead of making you retouch every screenshot or video elsewhere.

How It Works

The cleanup belongs to the frame, not a flattened export.

Clean Status Bar is stored with the frame overlay. It can be reflected in live preview, still export, video export, and `.bezel` project movement.

Best Use

Use it when raw screenshots or recordings already sit inside iPhone frames.

It pairs with device frames, captions, Canvas Motion, and batch export for cleaner launch visuals.
