Canvas Motion

Build animated App Store visuals with keyframes, easing, and per-item motion controls that stay inside the same canvas model.

Start building for free Back to all features

Animate the same canvas you already designed.

Canvas Motion turns static compositions into polished motion pieces without leaving the editor. You can keyframe individual elements, tune easing, and preview how the movement feels before exporting the final result.

The motion data lives with the canvas, which means it stays editable, syncable, and export-ready alongside your frames, text, backgrounds, and stickers.

How to use Canvas Motion

  • Select the item: Open a text layer, image, frame, or other supported overlay and switch into motion editing.
  • Add keyframes: Capture changes to position, scale, rotation, opacity, and visibility at the moments that matter.
  • Adjust the feel: Tune easing and timing so transitions land smoothly instead of looking robotic.
  • Preview in context: Watch the motion play directly on the canvas before exporting anything.
  • Export as video: Reuse the same motion data for preview assets or animated launch visuals.

Best for

  • App Store preview videos and motion-rich campaign assets.
  • Subtle product reveals, slide transitions, and premium movement.
  • Editing a scene once and reusing the motion across multiple canvases.
